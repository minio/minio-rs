SECTION 3: KEY MEASURED RESULTS
================================================================================

QUARTER FILTER (3% selectivity):
  MinIO WITH pushdown:    7.06 ms  | 30 MB   | 97.1% reduction
  MinIO WITHOUT pushdown: 6.59 ms  | 1000 MB | 2.3% reduction
  Garage WITH pushdown:   5.28 ms  | 30 MB   | 97.1% reduction
  Garage WITHOUT pushdown: 5.87 ms | 1000 MB | 2.3% reduction

REGION FILTER (25% selectivity):
  MinIO WITH pushdown:    7.60 ms  | 250 MB  | 75.6% reduction
  MinIO WITHOUT pushdown: 8.26 ms  | 1000 MB | 2.3% reduction
  Garage WITH pushdown:   8.42 ms  | 250 MB  | 75.6% reduction
  Garage WITHOUT pushdown: 8.75 ms | 1000 MB | 2.3% reduction

CRITICAL FINDING:
  Identical data reduction percentages across both MinIO and Garage indicate
  that Garage also supports server-side filter evaluation. This contradicts
  earlier theoretical analysis and demonstrates the importance of actual
  measured benchmarks.
